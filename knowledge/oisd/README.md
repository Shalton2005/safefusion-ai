# OISD

Placeholder folder for OISD (Oil Industry Safety Directorate) standards and guidelines.

No documents have been added yet. This folder holds a place in the corpus structure until legitimately sourced, licensed copies of the relevant OISD standards are available.

## Adding documents

- Only add documents you have the legal right to use. OISD standards are published by the Oil Industry Safety Directorate (Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas) — obtain copies through official OISD channels.
- Record the standard number (e.g. OISD-STD-xxx), title, revision/version, and retrieval date for each document added (see corpus [README.md](../README.md#provenance-tracking)).
